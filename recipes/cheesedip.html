Ingredients

  • Meat: This satisfying cheese dip starts with ground beef and ground pork sausage.
  • Onion: Cook the meat with a diced onion.
  • Cheese: You'll need a loaf of processed cheese food, such as Velveeta.
  • Tomatoes: A can of diced tomatoes and green chile peppers lends flavor and color.
  • Soup: A can of condensed cream of mushroom soup ensures a thick, creamy dip.

Steps

  1. Cook ground meat and onion in a hot skillet. Transfer meat into a slow cooker.
  2. Layer cheese cubes on the meat mixture.
  3. Blend canned tomatoes until smooth, then pour over the cheese with the soup.
  4. Cook on High until the cheese is completely melted.
